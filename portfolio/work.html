September 2019

An app icon created for my web design class. I went for a cloud storage app design, and actually used a variation of the icon for this website's logo.

January 2020

A flossing koala created for my New Media 2 class. I actually created this 2 days before the project deadline, as I was working on an animation with a rainforest setting, but was then told that koalas don't live in rainforests. Oops. I did gain some experience with Adobe Animate, however. Overall, I thought this animation was an absolute abomination holy sh- pretty cool.

November 2019

A caricature of myself based on the results and the art style of the Meyer-Briggs Personality Test. According to the test, I'm an "architect", one of the rarest personality types out there!

November 2019

A HUD redux for the game Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege. I'd mostly inverted their black with white accent and squared up (literally) many of the elements of the hud. Also, I played around with many variations of the elements in the bottom-left corner.

September 2019

A redesign of the Nintendo logo. I'd wanted a modern logo, but also wanted to include a callback to the NES. A demo video created in after-effects can be viewed on the associated Behance page (which I'm sure is copyrightable by Nintendo because it uses their footage but yolo I guess hahaha....)
